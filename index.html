Narrative Intelligence · Mantle Ecosystem

Read the narrative before it becomes consensus.

The Mantle Narrative Intelligence Agent turns scattered signal — X discussion, onchain & TVL data, ecosystem news, and prediction markets — into one structured, three-layer research verdict. Detect the dominant narrative, see what shifted, and forecast what comes next.

How it works
01

Feed the signals

Paste X discussion, onchain & TVL data, ecosystem news, and market / prediction data. The agent reasons over whatever you give it — no fixed schema, no dashboards to wire up.

02

The agent reads the room

Claude weighs social conviction against onchain flow, isolates the catalyst that moved sentiment, and measures where the story and the numbers diverge.

03

Get the verdict

A three-layer narrative report: the dominant narrative, the shift and its trigger, and the forward position if it holds — with an evidence matrix and a confidence score.
